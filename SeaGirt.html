Where I'm From

I am from Sea Girt NJ, a small, literally one square mile, town at the Jersey Shore. It’s a great place to escape from city life to go to the beach, ride a bike or take a walk in a beautiful spot. At the entrance to town there is a big sign that states, “Smile, You’re in Sea Girt.” It’s home to the The Parker House, a pretty famous summer bar. In fact, the New York Times suggested it beats the Hamptons. There is also a great Italian specialty store, Joe Leones, that makes the most delicious mozzarella sandwiches in the world.
